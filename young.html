8020 is a self-custodial wallet for token storage and transfers across major blockchains. It provides no exchange services. Users conduct token swaps independently via permissionless decentralized protocols, and 8020 can never access your assets.
I joined the project during my employment. As the UX Designer of the team, I led the overall UX Design of the app, and partnered with the engineering and marketing teams to deliver design alignment, functional testing and full product implementation.
Basing the design on clean and modern editorial aesthetics, I created a visually striking website that effectively communicates the security, transparency, and decentralized spirit of the 8020trade self-custodial wallet.
